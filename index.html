Anime & Manga Hub

A fan-made list of the best anime & manga websites and apps

Anilab (Android App)

Location: https://anilab.app

An Android anime streaming app with a clean interface, fast episode updates, and smooth playback. Popular among users who want a simple, app-based anime experience.

AnimeAppAndroid

HiAnime (Website)

Location: https://hianime.to

A widely used anime streaming website offering both subbed and dubbed anime. Known for its large library, frequent updates, and easy-to-use website design.

AnimeWebsiteSub & Dub

AniWave (Website)

Location: https://aniwave.to

A high-quality anime streaming website focused on fast servers, HD playback, and a wide selection of both classic and currently airing anime series.

AnimeWebsiteHD Streaming

Zoro / Aniwatch (Website)

Location: https://aniwatch.to

A popular anime streaming platform with a modern UI, watch history, and consistent episode updates. Great for binge-watching anime.

AnimeWebsiteUser-friendly

MangaFire (Website)

Location: https://mangafire.to

A fast-loading manga reading website with a huge collection of popular and lesser-known manga. Offers clean pages and multiple reading modes.

MangaWebsiteFast

MangaDex (Website)

Location: https://mangadex.org

A community-driven manga platform hosting fan translations in many languages. Highly respected for scanlation quality and official support options.

MangaWebsiteCommunity

Mihon (Android App)

Location: https://mihon.app

An open-source manga reader app that allows users to install extensions and read manga from multiple sources in one place. A successor-style app to Tachiyomi.

MangaAppOpen-source

Tachiyomi (Discontinued) (Android App)

Location: https://tachiyomi.org

A legendary open-source manga reader that inspired many forks and alternatives. Still widely used through community-maintained versions.
